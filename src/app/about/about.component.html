What is Modpool?

Modpool acts as a planner, course catalogue, knowledge bank and it provides module-related information such as community-driven module reviews and in future discussions.

Goals

Long term goal of Modpool is to enhance the quality students' lives at University of Liverpool.

You found an error?

If you think the error is caused by a bug in Modpool itself, please report it using email: modpool.info@gmail.com

Privacy

We care about your privacy! All of the reviews you leave are anonymous, so you can be honest, and help the community. We don't use any information for advertising, or share the information with anybody.
